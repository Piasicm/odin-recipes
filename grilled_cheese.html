Grilled Cheese

Home

Be ready to dive into this classic sandwhich. This grilled cheese recipe is as simple as it can get while
still tasting delicious. With only four ingredients, whether you are a beginner or a talented home cook,
this recipe will be one of the easiest to make. And if you don't like American cheese, then why not
substitue it for your favorite kind.

    Ingredients:

    Makes one serving

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • pepper to taste

    Steps

  1. Preheat a pan and butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice of bread on the pan with butter side down. Add cheese on top and pepper to taste.
  3. Put the second slice of bread on top, with butter side up.
  4. Cook until one side is golden brown, then flip over.
  5. Wait until the other side is golden brown and cheese is fully melted.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
