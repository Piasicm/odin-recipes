This recipe is sure to deliver you the iconic chocolate chip cookie that we all love. Go ahead and cook up some cookies wether for a special event or just for snacking these cookies will be sure to fit the occassion.

Ingredients

Makes 48 cookies

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspooon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspooon salt
  • 3 cups of all-pupose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • (optional) 1 cup of chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit(175 celsius)
  2. Beat and mix butter, brown, and white sugar in a bowl until smooth
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time and then add vanilla extract
  4. Dissolve baking soda in warm water and add to the mix along with the salt
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts until a soft dough forms
  6. Drop rounded spoonfuls of the dough onto an ungreased baking sheet
  7. Bake in the oven until golden brown on th edges, roughly 10 minutes
  8. Transfer onto wire rack and allow cookies to cool completely
  9. Serve and enjoy
